Tarpal category

Canvas

Canvas is a versatile, heavy-duty fabric known for its durability and strength. It is often used for making tents, sails, and as a painting surface. Its rugged nature makes it ideal for applications requiring sturdiness and longevity.

Plastic

Plastic tarpal is a waterproof and flexible material used for covering and protecting surfaces. It is commonly used in construction and agriculture due to its resistance to moisture, chemicals, and UV rays. Its lightweight nature makes it easy to handle and apply.

Net/Mesh

Net or mesh tarpal is a breathable material with a grid-like pattern. It is used for applications where ventilation and drainage are required, such as in agriculture for protecting crops or in construction for scaffolding. Its design allows for air flow while providing coverage.

PVC

PVC tarpal is a synthetic plastic material known for its strength and waterproof properties. It is used in various applications including outdoor coverings, awnings, and industrial uses. Its durability and resistance to weather conditions make it a popular choice for heavy-duty applications.
